RESEARCH PROJECTS

    
        
    
              
            

Virtual Agent Design to alleviate gender sterotypes in AI agents

              
                 Researched and identified problem space of AI conversation system through User Testing, Qualitative and Quantitative study and secondary research. Then ideate and created new design solution for 3D embodied agents. Validated and Iterated Design with expert review, quantitative and qualitative study. Finally, Evaluated the designed agents with research assumption by quantitative research method.             
                    
    
    
        
        
              
            

Smartphone App Design for early heart attack prediction

              
                 The research goal was to raise heart attack awareness on time in an easy way through available and accessible medium to mass people. I have visited multiple hospitals for 3 months to interview 70 hospitalized patients and taken in-person surveys of around hundred people. One of the foremost goal was to understand the problem space better to design an easy and effective mobile app.
